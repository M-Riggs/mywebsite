About me

I'm Michael, a senior software consultant at Improving. I've been developing full stack software using the Agile methodology since 2006 and consulting for nine of those years. I received my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from The University of Oklahoma in 2008, with a minor in mathematics. Outside of work, I spend my free time learning French, dancing, bicycling, playing video games, watching the ubiquitous Netflix, and playing with my dog Skye.

About

I'm Michael, a senior software consultant at Improving. I've been developing full stack software using the Agile methodology since 2006 and consulting for nine of those years. I received my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from The University of Oklahoma in 2008, with a minor in mathematics. Outside of work, I spend my free time learning French, dancing, bicycling, playing video games, watching the ubiquitous Netflix, playing with my dog Skye, and travelling.

Skillset

I have over a decade of Agile methodology experience over a wide range of industries including travel and leisure, lending, manufacturing, oil and gas, education, and more. During that time I've had the opportunity to work with many technologies, frequently having to pick up new ones quickly to meet project needs. Some of the technologies I've used include C#, .Net (Framework and Core), Entity Framework, Angular, Vue, Microsoft SQL Server, Selenium, CSS, Sass, Git, Subversion, and Node. Not to mention the host of other tools and frameworks to go along with them. I am also familiar with various best practices such as test driven development, continuous integration and deployment, use of design patterns, pair programing, and peer code review.
